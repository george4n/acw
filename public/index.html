Welcome to Amazing Weddings

A warm welcome to Amazing Cyprus Weddings where we can guide you from the first initial contact to a perfectly coordinated Wedding In Cyprus. We offer a wide variety of Wedding and Reception venues in Paphos along with meeting your personal requirements. You are free to choose simple wedding services and tailor your own day OR a wedding package to get you started complete with full blow wedding planning assistance. Let us know what you have in mind and how we can assist you and we can offer you the perfect Wedding In Cyprus service to suit your needs. We will guide you through the legalities right down to the finishing touches that will help enhance and create your Wedding In Cyprus. Regardless of the expected wedding party size, your budget or the wedding plans that you have in mind we will help you to simplify your wedding planning arrangements whilst reflecting the people you are in the ultimate celebration of your love. We take pride in personalising and perfecting each detail our services that will stand out from any other, it’s your wedding day so we don’t expect you to settle for anything less than what feels amazing to you. Your Wedding In Cyprus is only a click away!
